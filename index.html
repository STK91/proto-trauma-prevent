Welcome to Trauma Tetris

Fight Post-traumatic stress syndromes linked to the Covid19 pandemy

About this page

This contributory tool is used to develop methods to prevent the traumatic consequences of Covid19

The main goal is to test the comparative interest of Tetris, or standard psychological support such as CUMP or social support alone in the prevention of post-traumatic stress disorder in the context of sudden deaths and other traumas related to Covid19.
